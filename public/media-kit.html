About Us

Brooklyn Maids is a professional residential cleaning company serving Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and the Bronx. We deliver reliable, high-quality cleaning services backed by a satisfaction guarantee, licensed and insured staff, and a commitment to making every home spotless. Our team serves hundreds of homes each month and maintains a track record of consistent five-star service.
